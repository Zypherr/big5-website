About Us

Big 5 Esports is a player owned gaming organization with several of South Africa's best players competing at the highest level in the country. Our objective is to uphold the progression of esports, especially in South Africa, specifically growing the community by initializing player development projects lead by the founders, Directors, players and various members of the community to create nationwide awareness of esports.

Why Big 5 Esports?

We’re proud be apart of the esports growth with South Africa and to ne shaping part of its history with some of the top players in their respective titles. We’ve learned a lot along the way, and represent countless achievements by the talented Big 5 past. As we look to the future, we draw on the wealth of that history and knowledge—and challenge ourselves to build on it and reinvent.
